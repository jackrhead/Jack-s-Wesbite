My Favorite Shows

These are my top 3 favorite shows!

Naruto

Naruto was the first anime I ever watched and first watched it when I was in elementary school on Cartoon Network. I love all the characters, the plot, and the lessons taught throughout the show.

Here's a video of one of the best Naruto fights. Be sure to watch as long as you don't mind spoilers!

Attack On Titan

Attack On Titan has an evergrowing plot. It kept me guessing from day one with absolutely amazing animation and soundtrack. My favorite part of Attack on Titan is the intricicy of the plot and how there are no conventional "villains".

Hunter X Hunter

Hunter X Hunter is so wholesome yet has some very serious parts to it. It had me happy one episode and distraught in the next.
